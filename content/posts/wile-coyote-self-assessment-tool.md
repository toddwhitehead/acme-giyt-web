## Wile E. Coyote Leadership Self‑Assessment
Rate each statement from 1 (rarely true) to 5 (consistently true).

## Purpose Fixation
- I can articulate my primary objective in one clear sentence.
- My daily actions align directly with that objective.
- I regularly check for drift and correct it quickly.

## Iterative Mastery
- I treat failures as data.
- I adjust my approach based on learnings.
- I encourage experimentation in my team.

## Systems Orchestration
- I break complex problems into modular components.
- I understand dependencies and constraints.
- I design scalable, repeatable processes.

## Constraint Creativity
- I generate solutions even when resources are limited.
- I see constraints as opportunities.
- I adapt quickly when tools or conditions change.

## Resilient Neutrality
- I remain steady during high‑pressure situations.
- I avoid blame and focus on solutions.
- I reset quickly after setbacks.

## Accountability Loop
- I document decisions and assumptions.
- I own outcomes transparently.
- I share learnings openly.

## Ethical Persistence
- I pursue goals without compromising principles.
- I maintain boundaries under pressure.
- I model responsible behavior.

## Scoring Guide
- 30–35: Strong alignment.
- 22–29: Solid foundation.
- 15–21: Growth opportunity.
- Below 15: Revisit fundamentals.
